📊 Superstore Sales Analysis & Business Insights
🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes a retail Superstore dataset to uncover key insights into sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior. The goal is to identify trends and provide actionable recommendations to support data-driven business decisions.

📁 Dataset
Superstore retail dataset (sales transactions, product categories, regions, profit, discounts)
Contains 1000+ records of sales data
⚙️ Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas, NumPy
Matplotlib, Seaborn
Jupyter Notebook
📌 Key Objectives
Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)
Analyze sales and profit trends
Identify key drivers of profitability
Evaluate impact of discounts on profit
📈 Key Insights
Certain product categories contribute significantly more to overall profit
High discount levels negatively impact profit margins
Sales and profit vary across regions and customer segments
Seasonal trends influence overall sales performance
📊 Visualizations
Sales and profit trends over time
Category-wise and region-wise performance
Discount vs Profit analysis
Customer segment analysis
